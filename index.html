My Dream Job: Astronaut 👨‍🚀

Hi kids! I want to be an astronaut and fly to space!

🚀 What do astronauts do?

Astronauts explore space, walk on the Moon, and live in the International Space Station. They do science experiments and take pictures of Earth. It's super cool!

🚀 Fun Facts

  • Astronauts float in space because there is no gravity.
  • They wear special suits to breathe in space.
  • They eat ice cream that doesn't melt!

Astronauts train underwater to get ready for spacewalks.
